Terms & Conditions

  • Each commission usually takes around 2 days to complete. Please contact me beforehand if you have a specific deadline.
  • A 50% deposit is required before work begins. Deposits are non-refundable once the commission has started.
  • Unlimited revisions are available during the sketch stage, provided they stay within the original brief.
  • Up to 3 minor revisions are included during the line art and coloring stages. Additional or major revisions will incur an extra fee of 50 THB per revision.
  • The completed artwork will be delivered as a PNG file via email. Other file formats are available upon request.
  • Commercial use must be discussed and approved before work begins. Failure to disclose commercial usage in advance may result in the cancellation of the commission.
  • Any editing, modification, redistribution, tracing, reproduction, or use of my artwork for AI training, AI-generated content, NFTs, or machine learning is strictly prohibited under any circumstances.
